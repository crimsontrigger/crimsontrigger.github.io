A Study and Simulation for the Optimization of Robotic GMAW Process

I have worked on a project for the government organization Integral Coach Factory during my summer internship at their factory. This project is based on the real time optimization of the the welding process. It required a machine learning model which filtered and created an output of optimal weld parameters based on several inputs. It was an advanced structured data analysis problem that was made more difficult by the lack of consistent data. I was still able to achieve a accurate model which is the process of being implemented on the factory floor.
